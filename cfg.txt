Expr -> AdditiveExpr
AdditiveExpr -> MultiplicativeExpr AdditiveOperator AdditiveExpr | MultiplicativeExpr -Int | MultiplicativeExpr
    AdditiveOperator -> + | -
MultiplicativeExpr -> PrimaryExpr MultiplicativeOperator MultiplicativeExpr | PrimaryExpr
    MultiplicativeOperator -> * | /
PrimaryExpr -> Int | Float | ( Expr )


Expr types:
Int of n
Float of d
Binop of (op, expr, expr)
- Add
- Sub
- Mult
- Div
